## Step 6 — Post to Hacker News (Show HN)

**When:** Tuesday, 12:00 UTC (8am ET / 5am PT). This window had the best
results for AI dev tools in the data. Be awake to answer comments.

**Title (use this):**
`Show HN: Debt-Ops – catch AI-introduced tech debt as Claude writes it`

- Don't start with "I built." Spend the characters on the value, not yourself.

**Body (keep it ~200 words, problem first):**
- Open with the GitClear numbers (the smell, quantified).
- One sentence on what debt-ops does (registers deferrals as they happen).
- One sentence on the trust contract (refuses on auth/crypto/payments).
- One line: install is one command, no daemon.
- Link the repo and the demo GIF.
- End by inviting questions about the hook architecture or the ranking.

**On launch day:**
- Sit on the post from 5am–1pm PT.
- Reply to every top comment within ~90 minutes using your pre-written answers.
- Reply once per thread. Don't argue. Stay technical and friendly.
