# Days 21–60 — Amplify

Now spread the launch everywhere. Order matters: newsletters first (while
"trending on HN" is still true), Reddit a few days later (not same day as HN),
podcasts last (they need a number to quote).

---

## Step 8 — Submit to newsletters (Days 21–22)

While you can still say "trending on HN." Subject line template:
`Submission: Debt-ops — catches AI-introduced tech debt at write-time (trending on HN, [points])`

Top targets, in priority order:
- **Pragmatic Engineer** — reply to a Thursday "Pulse" issue. Best audience fit.
- **TLDR AI** — reply to the newsletter; AI Dev Tools section. Huge reach.
- **Latent Space** — pitch a *guest post*, not a link. (See Step 10.)
- **Bytes.dev**, **JavaScript Weekly**, **AlphaSignal**, **Ben's Bites** — link submissions.

> **Hand to an agent:**
> "Draft newsletter submission emails for the list in tmp/star-plan/03-amplify.md
> Step 8. One short pitch, customized per newsletter's section. Confirm current
> submission routes (reply-to vs. form vs. editor email)."

---

## Step 9 — Reddit (Days 25–35, staggered)

Not the same day as HN. Each sub wants a different format:
- **r/ClaudeAI** — show-and-tell with a screenshot of one real catch.
- **r/LocalLLaMA** — technical: the hook contract + refusal categories. Text
  post, not a bare link (bare links get auto-removed).
- **r/ExperiencedDevs** — a war story: "After 3 months of Claude PRs I wrote a
  plugin to catch what I kept missing." Repo link only in a comment.

Skip r/MachineLearning (wrong crowd) and r/programming for now (save it for the
data essay in Step 12).

> **Hand to an agent:**
> "Write 3 Reddit posts per the formats in tmp/star-plan/03-amplify.md Step 9.
> Read each sub's self-promo rules first and tell me how to stay onside."

---

## Step 10 — Latent Space guest post (Days 30–45)

Pitch a 1,500-word post: **"The hook-latency tax: lessons from a Claude Code
plugin."** This is amplifiable by Hamel, swyx, and Anthropic at once. Your
honest pitch ("the schema and the disciplines, packaged") plays well there.

> **Hand to an agent:**
> "Outline the Latent Space guest post in tmp/star-plan/03-amplify.md Step 10.
> Pull the real latency/design tradeoffs from docs/ and the plugin scripts."

---

## Step 11 — Podcast pitches (Days 40–55)

Only after you have an HN/star number. Long lead times; treat as compounding.
- **Latent Space** (swyx) — pair with the guest post.
- **The Changelog** / **Practical AI** — solo-maintainer story + trust contract.
- **Software Engineering Daily** — tech-debt-meets-AI angle.

> **Hand to an agent:**
> "Draft 4 short podcast pitch emails for the shows in tmp/star-plan/03-amplify.md
> Step 11. Each with a different angle and the right submission route."
